Neymar Jr

Full name: Neymar da Silva Santos Junior

Date of Birth: 5 February 1992

Current Team: Paris Saint-Germain

Current Age: 31

Father name: Neymar Santos Sr.

Mother nane: Nadine Goncalves

Neymar da Silva Santos Junior is a Brazilian football player with over 600+ G/A tally. He was born in a poor family at Mogi das Cruzes, Brazil but now is one of the highest paid athlete in the world and even holds the record for the highest transfer move of worth euro 222 million.

Neymar Jr. started his football career at a young age, joining the youth team of Santos FC in 2003. He quickly rose through the ranks and made his professional debut for the club in 2009, at the age of 17. Then because of his performance he was bought by barcelona in 2013. And, there in barcelona his performance rose even more and he was also able to win the most prestigious trophy i.e. the champions league trophy in 2015.

In 2017, Neymar Jr. made a world record transfer to Paris Saint Germain for euro 222 million and has been doing his exceptional things in the ligue1. In the international level, he still hasn't been able to win the world cup but was able to give brazil their first olympics gold medal in 2016. So, because of his amazing football career he is regarded as one of the best player of all time.
